Our Story

AgriAI was founded in 2023 by a team of agricultural engineers and AI experts who saw the need for affordable technology solutions for Indian farmers.

What We Do

We develop simple AI tools that help farmers:

  • Predict crop yields accurately
  • Identify plant diseases early
  • Get personalized farming advice
  • Track expenses efficiently

Our Mission

To make advanced farming technology accessible to every farmer in India, regardless of their education or technical skills.
